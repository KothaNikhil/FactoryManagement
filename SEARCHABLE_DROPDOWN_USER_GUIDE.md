## Troubleshooting

**Q: I typed "X" but nothing shows up**
A: No items start with "X". Press Escape to see all items, or try a different letter.

**Q: I accidentally typed wrong characters**
A: Press Backspace to delete characters one at a time.

**Q: I want to see all items again**
A: Press Escape to clear the search and display all items.

**Q: Can I use the mouse to scroll?**
A: Yes! After filtering, you can still scroll or click items in the dropdown list.
